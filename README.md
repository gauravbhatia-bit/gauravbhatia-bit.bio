### ⚡ [Geopolitical Market Impact Analyser — US-Iran Conflict & German Energy Markets](https://github.com/gauravbhatia-bit/iran-energy-impact-analyzer)
Analysed price impact of the **2026 US-Iran conflict** across German electricity (SMARD/EPEX), Dutch TTF gas, and Brent crude markets. Applied **event study methodology**, hypothesis testing (t-test, Cohen's d), and Cumulative Abnormal Return analysis — all results statistically significant (p < 0.05, Cohen's d > 0.8). Trained an **XGBoost model** with 26 engineered features for 90-day scenario forecasts. Quantified business impact: a 1% improvement in renewable forecast accuracy delivers **€8.2M/day** during crisis periods. Deployed as a 5-page interactive **Streamlit dashboard**.
